App Plan v2 — Supervisor & Accountant update

Updated after our meeting. Two big changes: Team Head is now the Supervisor (and he leads drivers too, not just workers), and there is a new Accountant role — ALL money goes through him. This document shows each role's powers in one diagram + one table, and the new money, diesel and material flows.

⚠️ Refined the same day — some rules on this page changed: supervisor's request now has NO cap (SUP-5/Q-5) · the SM's ₹1L ladder is gone — SM books any amount and the Accountant verifies EVERY money event (two-tick rule, Q-2/Q-3) · vehicle switch to an allowed type needs no approval at all (Q-4) · attendance is removed for every role · new: personal SALARY/PERSONAL khata, diesel bulk stock, per-type material settings, complaint box. The up-to-date complete plan: client-plan/finalindex.html · hub: client-plan/index.html
How to give your suggestions: every point has a code — like SUP-4 (Supervisor point 4) or ACC-1 (Accountant point 1) or Q-3 (a question waiting for your answer). Just tell us the code and your idea. Example: "SUP-5 — supervisor's limit should be ₹3,000."  |  "Q-1 — one accountant for the whole company."

1 · The 6 roles — who is who

Worker · मज़दूर

Works at the site. Sees only his own info. Requests small expenses. His ID card now carries his guardian's name & number.

Driver · ड्राइवर

Runs one vehicle. Daily morning/evening updates. Now also confirms the diesel he receives — his entry is matched against the supervisor's.

Supervisor · सुपरवाइज़र RENAMED — was Team Head

Field boss of workers AND drivers. Owns attendance, progress, materials and diesel issuing. Zero money powers.

Accountant · अकाउंटेंट ★ NEW

The money person. Approves every money request, any amount. Sees all finances — and only finances.

Site Manager · साइट मैनेजर

Head of one site. Sees everything on his site. Spends directly up to his limit. Only he can edit worker ID-card details.

Owner · मालिक

You. Everything the Site Manager sees + everything the Accountant sees — across all sites. Final word on anything.

MN-4 Vendor can also GIVE money ★ NEW

A local vendor you trust can hand the site money — e.g. ₹50,000 from the hardware vendor. The Accountant records it as money-IN; it shows in the vendor's khata and in your cash picture. Paying the vendor back is recorded the same way, so the vendor account always shows the true position: purchased on credit · money he gave us · money we paid him.

MN-5 "My debits" — everyone sees his own withdrawals ★ NEW

Any staff member — worker, driver, supervisor, site manager — opens his money page and sees a date-wise list of what he took from his earnings (to send home / personal use):

My debits10 Jul — ₹1,000  ·  06 Jul — ₹5,000  ·  28 Jun — ₹10,000

No more "how much did I take last month?" arguments — it is all there, for him and for you.
