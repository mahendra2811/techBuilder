1 · What this app is

A Hindi-first web app for a construction company’s daily field records — money, work progress, diesel, materials — with photo/voice proof, rolling UP to the Site Manager and Owner. A records + visibility logbook: nothing gets lost, nobody can argue about what happened.

2 · The 6 roles & the golden rules

RoleOne line
👑 Owner · मालिकSees everything, everywhere. Final word on anything.
🏗️ Site ManagerHead of ONE site. All site operations + finances visible. Books expenses directly.
🧮 AccountantThe money desk. Every rupee-event must carry his VERIFIED tick. Sees money only.
🪖 SupervisorField boss of a crew of workers + drivers. Progress, materials, diesel. Zero money powers.
🚛 DriverOne vehicle. Meter updates, diesel receipts, trips, damage.
👷 WorkerView-only + small requests. ID card carries his guardian's contact.
#Golden rule
1Data flows UP, never sideways.
2One-site sealed box. A Site Manager never sees another site. Only the Owner sees all boxes.
3Every money event is verified by the Accountant. Nothing auto-approves; approved + verified = permanent.
4SM-visible ⇒ Owner-visible. Always.
5Every worker/driver has exactly ONE supervisor, who sees only his own crew.
6No attendance in the app — for any role.

4 · The diesel double-check

Bulk stock → issue per vehicle → driver receipt → accountant match. Stock balance makes missing diesel visible.

5 · Materials

The SM creates each material type with its own on/off settings for who fills what.

6 · Vehicles

SituationWhat happens
Switch to an allowed vehicle typeNo request. Driver logs "changed to vehicle B" → Supervisor + SM notified. Done.
Switch to a non-allowed typeRequest → SM decides; Supervisor informed.
Morning (compulsory)Meter photo + start reading, every working day.
Evening (optional)Closing reading · hours · trips/loads · note.
DamageDriver raises (photos/voice) → SM resolves with remark → driver may close. Kept forever on the vehicle's page.
Vehicle insightsAverages (7/30/90-day), diesel per vehicle, monthly cost — SM + Owner ONLY.
Documents & reminders (NEW — this phase)Per-vehicle vault: insurance, PUC, RC, permit, fitness — any PDF/photo — each with its expiry date, plus EMI dues. Reminders X days before a date, or monthly/yearly → 🔔 notification. Upload & view: SM + Owner ONLY — no other role even sees these exist.
